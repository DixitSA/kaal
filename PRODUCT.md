# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Mixed audience of spiritual seekers and practical optimizers using Kaal on mobile and desktop.

Spiritual seekers approach with contemplative mood, seeking Vedic wisdom for timing rituals, life phases, and spiritual alignment. Practical optimizers want actionable timing signals for business and life decisions — they're pragmatic but open to ancient systems.

Primary context: quick daily check-ins (Today section), deeper phase exploration, and decision support. Users are in a reflective, intentional frame of mind — not rushing, seeking clarity.

## Product Purpose

Kaal delivers deterministic Vedic astrology insights through a sophisticated interface. It turns chart inputs into daily timing signals, phase guidance, and decision support — all jargon-light and actionable.

Success looks like: users building a daily ritual around checking their signal, trusting Kaal's timing wisdom for meaningful decisions, and upgrading to Pro for uninterrupted access.

## Brand Personality

Editorial, luxe, curated

Think high-end spiritual wellness — Calm or Headspace but more sophisticated, with earthy luxury. Not mystical fluff, not corporate efficiency. Tactile, timeless, authoritative but warm.

## Anti-references

**New-age clichés to avoid:**
- Bright purple, neon gradients, starfield backgrounds
- Crystal clipart, zodiac stock photography
- Mystical tropes, overly ornate decorations

**Corporate SaaS to avoid:**
- Corporate blue, stock photos, generic templates
- Card-heavy dashboards, default material design
- Sterile efficiency, soulless minimalism

## Design Principles

1. **Show, don't tell** — Let the daily signal speak through editorial presentation, not explanatory text
2. **Earthy luxury over mystical theater** — Rich textures and warm tones, not neon spirituality
3. **Practice what you preach** — The interface embodies the calm, intentional timing it teaches
4. **Expert confidence** — Authoritative voice without arrogance, like a seasoned Vedic counselor

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA compliance required:
- Color contrast ratios ≥ 4.5:1 for normal text, ≥ 3:1 for large text
- Keyboard navigation support
- Screen reader friendly markup
- Respect prefers-reduced-motion
